PromptKey

Local dictation across your Mac.

PromptKey is a local push-to-talk dictation app for macOS. Hold a key, speak naturally, and send text back to the app you were already using.

Read Privacy Policy Support

What PromptKey does

Works across editors, browsers, notes apps, chat apps, and terminal workflows.

Keeps speech recognition local on your Mac instead of routing transcription through a hosted speech-to-text service.

Uses clipboard fallback when switching apps mid-dictation so text does not end up in the wrong place.
